about me

Bio:

Layne Hutchings is a freelance 3D artist with 5 years of experience working with small to large scale animation houses. Layne specializes in 3D character animation but is also passionate about creating hard surface models and texturing.

Layne works exeptionally well with teams and does not fear encroaching deadlines, these only make him work harder and bring the most out of his team to put out the best work they can. Layne gets his motivation from the thought of a polished product when the project is over.

In his free time, Layne is a racing driver at the local race track, he likes to run , cycle and play video games with his brother.

Education:

During high school I went to multiple short courses revolving around 3D character animation and 3D modeling. After High School I enrolled for a 3 year course at The Animation School.

Recently I have been doing Udemy courses to do with coding. As of 9th September I have enrolled at Hyperion Dev and am partaking in the 6 month Immersive Full-Stack Developer Bootcamp.
